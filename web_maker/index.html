05 — Design Concept

Design language
built for infinity.

Every pixel Meta creates is governed by a single philosophy: design for where people are going, not where they are.

Visual Identity

The Meta logo — a Möbius-strip infinity symbol — is designed to exist in three-dimensional space, traversable and textured. It connects the company's future to Facebook's legacy blue through a gradient that shifts from #0668E1 to lighter tones.

Typography — Custom rounded sans-serif (Meta Optimistic). Minimal stroke contrast, generous x-height, weight range 300–800. No decorative fonts; clarity is law.

Core Principles

  1. 01

    Simplicity

    Remove friction. Strip UI to essentials. Content-first, zero decoration.

  2. 02

    Adaptive Systems

    Brand flexes across 2D, AR overlays, VR environments, and wearables.

  3. 03

    Hierarchy & Grid

    8pt grid, clear typographic weight, layered information architecture.

  4. 04

    Accessibility First

    WCAG 2.1 AA baseline. Designed for mobility, vision, and hearing diversity.

  5. 05

    Spatial-Ready

    Every component considers depth, shadow, and parallax for Horizon OS.
